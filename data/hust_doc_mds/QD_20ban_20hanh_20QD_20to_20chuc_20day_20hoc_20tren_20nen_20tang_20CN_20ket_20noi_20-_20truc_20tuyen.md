## Dieu 4. Dieu kien thyc hien B-Learning

Cac yeu cau doi voi giang vien/nhom chuyen mon de thyc hien B-Learning bao gom:

1. Ngoai cac yeu cau co ban doi voi giang vien turong ty nhu giang day tryc tiep, giang vien can sur dung thanh thao cac churc nang co ban cua LMS, sir dung duoc cac churc nang nang cao cua LMS ve quan ly khoa hoc, giai dap/thao luan, kiém tra danh gia nguoi hoc.
2. De cuong hoc phan phai dam bao m6i lien két chat che gitra noi dung giang day tryc tiép va noi dung bai giang dien tur tren LMS. Ké hoach, lich trinh giang day phai phan dinh r6 ve noi dung, thoi luong cua cac tiet hoc giang day tryc tiép va truyen phat tren LMS, gan ket voi cach thurc kiém tra, danh gia nguoi hoc theo tung noi dung kién thurc. Thoi luong kién thurc duoc truyen phat tren LMS toi da la 70% thoi lurong cua hoc phan. Diem qua trinh co trong s6 toi thieu la 0,3 khi tinh diem hoc phan.

3. Bai giang dien tur truyen phat tren LMS (sau day goi chung la bai giang B-Learning) phai dap ung yeu cau va dupc phe duyet, cho phep trien khai giang day. Cac yeu cau toi thiéu doi voi mot bai giang B-Learning duoc quy dinh chi tiet trong Phu luc 1.
4. C6 hoc lieu s6 bo tro ngoai bai giang B-Learning, bao gom cac tai lieu hoc tap theo dinh dang file PDF hoac PPT va cac hinh thurc khac nham ho tro nguoi hoc ty hoc tap va nghien curu.
5. Ngoai giang vien duroc phan cong giang day, don vi chuyen mon phai co ke hoach tuyen chon va b6 tri toi thiéu mot (01) tro ly hoc tap (sinh vien nam cuoi, hoc vien cao hoc, NCS) d6i voi cac lop hoc phan c6 qui mo tur 40 nguoi hoc tr? lén. Tro ly hoc tap duroc tap huan nghiep vu, thong hiéu noi dung cua hoc phan va co nhiem vu ho tro giang vien trong viec chuan bi bai giang, huong dan bai tap, thao luan, cham bai tap, theo doi, quan ly qua trinh hoc tap cua sinh vien.

## PHU LUC

(kem Quy dinh ve to chirc hoat dong day-hoc tren nen tang cong nghe ket noi va trurc tuyen, ban hanh theo Quyét dinh s0 1515/QD-DHBK-DT ngay 29 thang 6 nam 2021 cuia Hieu truong Truong Dai hoc Bach khoa Ha noi)

Phu luc 1: Cac yeu cau co ban dbi voi bai giang B-Learning

1. Bai giang B-Learning gom mot hoac mot so video dupc sap xep theo noi dung giang day (cac video bai giang), phai dap ung cac yeu cau ky thuat co ban sau day:
- a) Hinh anh HD 720p hoac Full HD 1080p theo ti le khung hinh 16:9, dam bao nhin hinh anh ro nét va khong l6a;
- b) Am thanh dinh dang Mp3 chat lurong 128Kbps hoac 320Kbps, dam bao nghe ro va khong bi nhieu tieng;
- c) Dung luong toi da 400 MB /video.
2. Thoi lurong video bai giang:
- a) Quy chuan thoi luong bai giang B-Learning (tinh theo so phut truyen phat) theo nguyen tac: thoi lurong video bai giang trong khoang 15-30 phut tuong duong voi 45 phut day-hoc tryc tiép tren lop, tuy thuoc noi dung giang day hoc phan. Thoi gian con lai cua tiét hoc danh cho nguoi hoc tyr lam cac bai trac nghiem ngan (quiz) hoac tra loi cac cau hoi de hiéu, nam bat tot hon noi dung cua bai hoc.
- b) Phan chia video bai giang thanh nhom cac video clip. Moi video clip trong nhom gan voi mot chu de kién thurc de nguoi hoc de theo doi va tiép thu voi thoi lurong lien tuc khong hoi kiem tra kién thuc mot cach phu hop.
3. Cac yeu cau co ban ve thiét ke noi dung trinh chiéu trong video bai giang:
- a) Cau truc noi dung trinh chiéu bai giang phai bao gom mot so trang trinh chiéu co ban (néu co sur dung cong cu trinh chiéu), bao gom: Trang thong tin chung (ten hoc phan/bai giang, ten giang vien/nhom chuyen mon, don vi); trang muc luc va muc tieu cua bai giang; cac trang noi dung chi tiét cua bai giang; trang tong ket bai giang va gioi thieu noi dung hoc tap tiép theo.
- b) Siur dung loai font chu khong chan nhu Arial, Helvetica voi co chu toi thieu turong duong chur Arial co 18.
4. Cac yeu cau ve hinh anh cua nguoi giang va loi thoai:
- a) Hinh anh cua giang vien xuat hien tren video bai giang voi phong thai ty tin, thoai mai, than thien de tao sy truyen cam va thu hut sy tap trung cua nguoi hoc. Tan suat, thoi gian xuat hien tuy thuoc vao cach thurc truyen dat (thuyét minh cac trang trinh chiéu hay thuyet giang voi bang).
- b) Loi thoai phai ro rang, ty nhien va c6 noi dung gan két voi noi dung bai giang.
5. Cac video bai giang duoc thyc hien tryc tiép tren lop hoc sau khi xur ly hau ky phai dap ung cac yeu cau ve chat luong am thanh, hinh anh, thoi lurong tai muc 1, 2 va 3 cua Phu luc nay de durgc xet duyet dura vao sur dung cho B-Learning.

6. Quy cach cua bai giang B-Learning do Truong dat hang va giao nhiem vy co yeu cau chat che hon ve hinh thurc va noi dung theo huong dan chi tiet cua Ban dieu phoi hoat dong doi moi phuong phap dao tao.

## Phu luc 2. Quy trinh dang ky, bien soan de curong hoc phan, xay dung bai giang, to churc tham dinh bai giang B-Learning

1. Vao tuan dau hoc ky chinh, Ban dieu phoi hoat dong doi moi phuong phap dao tao (sau day goi tat la Ban dieu phoi) to churc tiép nhan dang ky hoc phan se chuan bi cac dieu kien trien khai giang day theo hinh thuc B-Learning trong hoc ky chinh ke tiép.
2. Giang vien/nhom chuyen mon (sau day goi chung la giang vien) hoan thanh De cuong hoc phan (DCHP) phu hop voi hinh thuc B-Learning, guri Ban dieu ph6i tong hop, cham nhat la tuan thu 3 cua hoc ky.
3. Hoi dong chuyen mon do Hieu truong thanh lap to churc tham dinh DCHP, can cur quy dinh tai Dieu 4 cua Quy dinh nay.
4. Giang vien co DCHP dat yeu cau se duroc h6 tro ky thuat, nghiep vu (néu can) dé xay dung bai giang B-Learning theo cac buoc sau:
- a) Giang vien guri noi dung trinh chiéu da xay dung cho 1-2 bai giang dau tien de duroc Ban dieu phoi tu van, ho tro chinh sua, cai tién cho dung quy cach.
- b) Giang vien dang ky lich quay hinh/thu am video clip tai cac phong ky thuat/truong quay chuyen dung cua Truong de dugc ho tro ky thuat (quay phim, hau ky) va thyc hien theo lich da dang ky.
5. Giang vien hoan thanh toan bo bai giang B-Learning theo DCHP, guri Ban dieu ph6i tong hop, cham nhat la 02 thang truoc hoc ky moi.
6. Hoi dong chuyen mon to churc tham dinh ve quy cach, hinh thurc va chat lurong cua bai giang truoc thoi diem m? dang ky lop hoc phan cua hoc ky ké tiép, dam bao c6 du thoi gian trien khai giang day theo hinh thurc B-Learning trong hoc ky ke tiép doi voi cac hoc phan co bai giang dat yeu cau.

3. Phong Dao tao guri Trung tam Mang thong tin danh sach cac lop hoc phan theo hinh thurc B-learning, kem danh sach sinh vien cac lop de chuan bi dur liéu cac khoa hoc tren LMS.
